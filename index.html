About the Temple

About Veesundaramalai Arulmigu Sri Muthumari Amman Aalayam

Veesundaramalai Arulmigu Sri Muthumari Amman Aalayam is a sacred Hindu temple nestled in the heart of our village, dedicated to the divine service of the community for centuries. The temple stands as a symbol of faith, unity, and spiritual solace for devotees from the village and surrounding areas.

Daily pujas, weekly bhajans, and annual festivals are conducted with great devotion and adherence to ancient Vedic traditions. The temple is not just a place of worship but a vibrant center for cultural and spiritual activities that bring the community together.

We warmly invite you to visit the temple, seek blessings, and experience the divine tranquility that pervades this holy place.

Our Heritage

History of the Temple

The origins of Veesundaramalai Arulmigu Sri Muthumari Amman Aalayam trace back many years, deeply rooted in the spiritual and cultural fabric of our village. According to local lore, the sacred Sri Muthumari Amman idol was discovered under divine circumstances, and the temple was established by the village elders with the blessings of great saints.

Over the centuries, the temple has been lovingly maintained and renovated by successive generations of villagers. The architecture reflects traditional Dravidian style, with intricate carvings and a serene sanctum that radiates divine energy.

Today, the temple continues to be the spiritual heartbeat of our community, preserving ancient traditions while embracing devotees from all walks of life.
